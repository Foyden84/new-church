Our Services

Join us as we gather, worship, and grow together in faith.

Weekly Services

We offer various opportunities to worship, learn, and connect.

Sunday Worship

8:00 AM & 10:00 AM

Experience passionate worship, encouraging messages, and a welcoming environment for all generations.

Kids & Youth

Sundays & Wednesdays

Dynamic programs for children and youth to grow in faith, friendship, and fun.

Midweek Small Groups

Various Times

Connect, study God's word, and build lasting relationships in a small group throughout the week.

What to Expect on Sundays

8:00 AM Traditional Service

  • Classic hymns and traditional worship
  • Choir-led music
  • Scripture-focused teaching
  • Communion on the first Sunday of each month
  • Approximately 75 minutes in length

10:00 AM Contemporary Service

  • Modern worship band and contemporary music
  • Multimedia presentations
  • Practical, relevant teaching
  • Communion on the first Sunday of each month
  • Approximately 75 minutes in length

For Both Services:

  • Children's ministry available for infants through 5th grade
  • Coffee and refreshments in the lobby
  • Friendly greeters to help you find your way
  • No dress code - come as you are!

Children & Youth Ministries

Nurturing the faith of the next generation.

Grace Kids (Ages 0-11)

Our children's ministry provides age-appropriate Bible teaching, worship, crafts, and activities in a safe, fun environment. Available during both Sunday services.

  • Nursery (0-2 years)
  • Preschool (3-5 years)
  • Elementary (K-5th grade)

All children's workers are background-checked and trained in child safety procedures.

Youth Ministry (Ages 12-18)

Our youth ministry helps teens grow in their faith through relevant teaching, worship, small groups, and fun activities.

  • Middle School (6th-8th grade): Sundays at 10:00 AM
  • High School (9th-12th grade): Sundays at 10:00 AM
  • Youth Group: Wednesdays 6:30-8:00 PM

Special events, retreats, and service projects throughout the year.

Small Group Ministry

Connect with others in a more intimate setting for growth and fellowship.

Small groups are the heart of our church community. Meeting in homes throughout the week, these groups provide a place to build relationships, study Scripture, pray together, and support one another.

Types of Small Groups:

Life Stage Groups

Groups for singles, young married couples, parents, empty nesters, and seniors.

Interest-Based Groups

Groups focused on specific topics like parenting, finances, or specific Bible studies.

Neighborhood Groups

Groups organized by geographic location to foster community in your area.
