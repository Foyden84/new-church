About Grace Community Church

Our mission is to love God, love people, and build disciples. Founded in 1993, our church family has grown into a vibrant, multi-generational community rooted in the teachings of Jesus Christ. We believe in transforming lives through worship, teaching, service, and authentic relationships.

What We Believe

Our core beliefs and values that guide our ministry and community.

Our Core Beliefs

  • We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • We believe in the deity of Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, miracles, atoning death, bodily resurrection, and ascension.
  • We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God.
  • We believe in salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ.
  • We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit, empowering believers to live godly lives.
  • We believe in the spiritual unity of believers in Christ.
  • We believe in the return of Christ and the resurrection of believers.
