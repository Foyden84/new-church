Why We Give

Your generosity makes a difference in our church and community.

Biblical Stewardship

We believe that everything we have comes from God, and giving is an act of worship and obedience. The Bible teaches us to give cheerfully and generously, not out of obligation but from grateful hearts.

"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver."

- 2 Corinthians 9:7

Through your faithful giving, you partner with us in sharing the love of Christ and making a lasting impact in our community and beyond.

Your Giving Makes a Difference

  • Ministry Operations

    Supports our weekly services, staff, and facility maintenance.

  • Children & Youth Programs

    Provides resources for nurturing the faith of the next generation.

  • Community Outreach

    Funds local service projects, food pantry, and assistance programs.

  • Global Missions

    Supports missionaries and international humanitarian efforts.

  • Building & Expansion

    Helps maintain and improve our facilities for ministry growth.

Financial Accountability

At Grace Community Church, we are committed to financial integrity and transparency. We take seriously the responsibility of stewarding the resources entrusted to us.

Oversight

Our finances are overseen by a qualified finance team and elder board who ensure responsible management.

Transparency

Annual financial reports are available to church members, showing how funds are allocated and used.

Auditing

We conduct regular financial reviews and audits to maintain the highest standards of accountability.

If you have questions about our church finances or giving options, please contact our finance team at finance@gracechurch.org.
